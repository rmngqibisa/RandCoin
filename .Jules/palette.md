## 2024-10-27 - [Mining Feedback Enhancement]
**Learning:** In CLI applications, providing immediate visual feedback (emojis) and performance metrics (time taken) significantly improves the perception of "work being done" and system responsiveness, even for synchronous blocking operations.
**Action:** For future CLI tasks, always include a "start" indicator (like "Mining...") and a detailed "summary" (Time, Result, etc.) upon completion for long-running processes.
